VOA Process

Once payment is received you will receive your receipt with your client number. You will need to use your client number to upload and email the following documents in order to process your VOA Approval Letter.

  1. Copy of Passport Data Page
  2. Copy of your flight itinerary
  3. Passport Photo (headshot of you with white background)

Once you've been approved, you'll receive a notification of your approval. However, you will receive your official approval VOA letter from the Nigerian Embassy within 5 days of your flight. This is done in case there are any changes to your flight, since your letter will have your identifying flight information.

Important!

You'll need this letter to board your plane.

Once you arrive in Lagos, Nigeria your Immigration Officer will greet you and then escort you to the Immigration Office to receive your Visa and Entry Stamp.

Once you have your Visa you'll be escorted to retrieve your luggage and assisted through Customs. Your loved one will be waiting for you right outside of the Security Doors.

  2. If you're making a security deposit of $100*.

*Security Deposit is non-refundable. Any remaining balance will be given as credit.
